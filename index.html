Echoes Of The Past (2025)
PC | videogame

Echoes of the Past is a visually striking narrative puzzle game developed in Unity by a multidisciplinary team. Players uncover a family mystery through their mother's glasses, solving interactive puzzles and experiencing story moments through in-game animations.

Kapsula 12 (2025)
Interactive informative tool

Kapsula 12 is an interactive project built in Unity HDRP that lets users examine a futuristic escape capsule from every angle. Through high-fidelity visuals and intuitive tools like progressive slicing, exploded views, and part-by-part inspection, users get to uncover the capsule’s inner workings.
