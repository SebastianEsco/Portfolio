About Me

I'm Sebastián Escobar, a digital creator focused on interactive storytelling. My journey began with a fascination for how technology can evoke emotion and spark curiosity.

I specialize in Unity development, building experiences for PC, mobile, virtual reality (VR), and augmented reality (AR). I'm driven by how design, code, and imagination come together to create meaningful interactions.

Beyond the technical, I'm deeply inspired by art, architecture, and the human stories behind technology.

Echoes of the Past(2025)
PC | Videogame

Echoes of the Past is a visually striking narrative puzzle game developed in Unity by a multidisciplinary team. Players uncover a family mystery through their mother's glasses, solving interactive puzzles and experiencing story moments through in-game animations.

Kapsula 12 (2025)
Interactive Informative Tool

Kapsula 12 is an interactive project built in Unity HDRP that lets users examine a futuristic escape capsule from every angle. Through high-fidelity visuals and intuitive tools like progressive slicing, exploded views, and part-by-part inspection, users get to uncover the capsule’s inner workings.

Entre Balcones (2025)
VR | Interactive Experience

A VR - Hand Tracked journey through community stories, photography, and the living memory of a Colombian neighborhood.

Nuclear Rush (2024)
