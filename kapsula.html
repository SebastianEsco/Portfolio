Kapsula 12

Interacive informative tool | 2025

An immersive 3D visualization experience exploring the design, structure, and secrets of a survival capsule

Kapsula 12 is an interactive project built in Unity HDRP that lets users examine a futuristic escape capsule from every angle. Through high-fidelity visuals and intuitive tools like progressive slicing, exploded views, and part-by-part inspection, players uncover the capsule’s inner workings and its role in an unknown planetary mission.

About the project

Kapsula 12 is a visually rich interactive visualization built in Unity HDRP, designed with a realistic yet stylized aesthetic. The project leverages the power of HDRP to deliver high-quality materials and shaders that enhance clarity and understanding of the capsule’s structure, while maintaining interactivity and smooth performance. Post-processing is present but subtle, with the main visual focus on custom shaders crafted to support informative and intuitive exploration.

Users navigate the capsule by interacting with UI elements or directly manipulating camera points of interest, enabling three distinct exploratory journeys through the exterior, interior, and individual components. Although primarily a technical visualization, the experience includes contextual narrative elements to engage the user.

Developed in about one month as part of a visualization and information design course, the team faced the challenge of balancing advanced visual tools with fluid interactivity. Through this project, I honed skills in writing clean, organized code with well-managed state machines and efficient use of Unity Events to facilitate scalable, maintainable systems. This approach was essential to ensuring seamless integration of complex visual features without compromising performance or usability.

My role in the development

I led programming for the capsule’s exterior systems, including slicing, piece explosion, part visualization, and UI integration. I also modeled and textured the capsule’s exterior and fully developed one of the internal puzzles with responsive interactive screens. Throughout, I focused on writing clean, scalable code using Unity Events to ensure flexibility and team collaboration, while mastering HDRP workflows for shaders and camera control.

What I took away from this project

Kapsula 12 allowed me to strengthen my ability to structure scalable systems and write clean, maintainable code in a highly visual project. I gained valuable experience using Unity HDRP, learning how to manage materials, shaders, and camera behaviors unique to this pipeline. The project also helped me refine my use of Unity Events to create flexible connections between systems and improve team collaboration. Overall, it reinforced the importance of combining technical precision with visual clarity to create informative yet engaging interactive experiences.
